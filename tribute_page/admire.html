Biography

Mahendra Singh Dhoni, also known as MS Dhoni, is an Indian cricketer who is widely regarded as one of the greatest wicket-keeper batsmen and captains in the history of cricket. He captained the Indian national team from 2007 to 2017 and is known for his calm demeanor, sharp leadership, and strategic acumen. Under his captaincy, India won several prestigious tournaments, including the ICC T20 World Cup in 2007, the ICC World Cup in 2011, and the ICC Champions Trophy in 2013.

Off the field, Dhoni is known for his humility, dedication, and inspirational leadership, making him a role model for millions of fans around the world.

Achievements

  • ICC T20 World Cup Winner (2007)
  • ICC World Cup Winner (2011)
  • ICC Champions Trophy Winner (2013)
  • Most Successful Indian Captain in Test Cricket
  • First Indian Captain to achieve No. 1 Test ranking
